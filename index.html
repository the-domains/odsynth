Reaction Field | a Processing Installation with GreyScott reaction diffusion

In drama, students are trained to sense the presence of others on stage and react through the invisible touches. This experiment attempt to simulate a person/an object's disturbance on the Xi/Aura of a room. An infrared camera captures the disturbance on stage and a "force field" is simulated/fauxed on the trail of movement.
